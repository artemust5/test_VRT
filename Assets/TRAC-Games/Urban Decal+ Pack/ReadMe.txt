TRAC GAMES (http://www.trac-group.co.nz/games) provides high quality game assets for the Unity3d Game Engine to help make your games look great and save you time!

Welcome to the Urban Decal+ Pack. Thank you for purchasing this pack we hope you enjoy it.

Included in this pack are 38 High Res Urban Decals to add detail to your street scenes!
Also included are some low poly roadside kerbs to help build your scene quickly.

Details: Most decals are mapped to a single quad, kerbs use two quads.

Texture: Diffuse, Specular (alpha channel for some) and Normal maps for most at 2048x2048 and 1024x1024 (you can reduce map size as required in Unity3d).

To use these assets, drag out the prefabs as required. Take a look at the example scene and press play to see the assets laid out in an environment.
